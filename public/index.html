Where signals go

Sina Gateway routes inquiries across named venture lanes — not a black hole form.

  • SourceA — governed AI execution and agentic work with control.
  • Noetfield — strategic partnership and venture design.
  • TrustField — trust, governance, and compliance pressure.
  • BuildMatch — Vancouver construction and home-services leads.
  • Forge — builders, tools, and collaboration energy.
  • Personal — friends, warm intros, and network context.
  • Founder Audit — solo founders (campaign link or notes signal).

What happens after you send

  1. You receive an on-screen confirmation with a reference ID and routed lane.
  2. The signal is captured securely for review (live database — not a placeholder).
  3. Sina reviews in priority order and follows up on the contact you provide.

High-priority signals alert the operator channel. Email confirmation is not sent unless explicitly added later.
