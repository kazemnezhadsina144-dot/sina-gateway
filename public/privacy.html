Sina Gateway

Privacy

How inquiries submitted through Sina Gateway are used.

What we collect

The intake form asks for identity, intent, contact details, and optional business context so your inquiry can be sorted to the right product line for review.

How we use it

Details are used for sorting, review, and follow-up only. They are stored in a secured database and are not sold. Access is limited to the operator reviewing the relevant product line.

Consent

Submit only business or referral inquiries you are authorized to share. Use the consent checkbox on the form before sending.

Cookies and tracking

Sina Gateway does not use advertising or cross-site tracking cookies. The intake form may store a local draft and anonymous session identifiers in your browser so the wizard can restore progress. Cloudflare Turnstile may set cookies when bot protection is enabled on the contact step.

Deletion

To request deletion of a submission, contact the operator through the same channel you used to reach this gateway and include your reference ID from the confirmation screen.
