**1 · Your decisions.** The material calls you've made recently, what you
expected from each, and which ones you never went back to check. Most

**2 · Your commitments.** What you said you'd do vs what happened — your
real follow-through rate, and the pattern behind the misses.

**3 · Your offer.** What you're actually selling, in whose words, to which
audience — and whether it has changed shape every time someone gave you
feedback. (It usually has.)

**4 · Your people pipeline.** Everyone circling your company — potential
partners, investors, advisors — sorted by *evidence*: who has actually done
something, and who has only talked.

**5 · The install.** We don't leave you with a PDF. We set up your personal
ledger system — decisions, commitments, offer, people — and a daily
