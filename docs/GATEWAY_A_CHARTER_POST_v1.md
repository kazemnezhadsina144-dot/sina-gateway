# What @Gateway_A actually does

**Channel charter (public post copy)**

@Gateway_A is the ops and business channel for **Sina Gateway** — not a live chat bot, not a company feed, not a marketing blast list.

## What shows up here

- **Urgent inquiries** — high-priority leads with product line, confirmation code, and routing context (no full PII dump).
- **Infra verdicts** — when the gateway or database is degraded.
- **Lane aggregates** — weekly counts by product line when motion is active (no names).
- **Commercial heartbeat** — outbound send/reply counters only when founder has armed real outreach.

## What this is not

- 24/7 chat support (one operator, human schedule).
- Fake uptime percentages or subscriber milestones.
- Alerts from NOOS, SourceA loops, or unrelated venture probes.
